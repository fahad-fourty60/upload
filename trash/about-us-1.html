Claiming Shares Lodged with IEPF

If the dividends declared by a Company remain unclaimed for seven consecutive years, then the dividends as well as the shares are transferred to IEPF. The process to claim shares from IEPF is very complicated and no individual shareholder would be able to complete the formalities. This is where we come in. We have the contacts, experience, knowhow and success record of unlocking tremendous value for our clients. We can do the same for you.

Ensuring All Legal Heirs Receive Their Due Share of the Funds Recovered

If the person whose name is on the shares has died, we examine all legal documents to identify the true heirs. This can involve wills and probate (if any), identification of all possible heirs and finally consent terms to ensure that the funds are not contested.
